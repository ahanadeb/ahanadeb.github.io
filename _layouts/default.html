Ahana Deb (অহনা দেব)

I am a first second year PhD student at the AI-ML group, part of the School of Engineering at Universitat Pompeu Fabra, where I work on Reinforment Learning. My PhD advisor is Anders Jonsson.

Previously, I did my Master's in Intelligent Interactive Systems at UPF, and have a Bachelor's in Instrumentation and Electronics Engineering from Jadavpur University, India.

Currently I'm working on offline learning of Regular Decision Processes and Automata Learning, and looking for ideas to exapnd it to the online setting.
